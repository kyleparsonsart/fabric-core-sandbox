Northstar toggle

One switch applies all 475 v1→v2 semantic mappings live on the page. Flip it back to restore.

🎛

Per-token override

Click any token to open the primitive swatch picker. Choose from 12 color ramps to override individual values.

🔗

Share overrides

The Share button encodes your current remapping to a URL hash — paste it to send teammates an exact preview state.

📋

CSS export

Export a :root { } block of all your overrides, ready to paste into any stylesheet.

Sharing a preview state

Hit Share in the panel footer — the URL updates to include a #tnsp=… hash encoding all your current overrides. Copy and paste the URL to send teammates an exact preview state. They'll see the same remapping when they open the panel on the same page.
